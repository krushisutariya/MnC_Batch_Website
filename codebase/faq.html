Computer Science focuses on computers, dealing with how they work, using algorithms, programming languages, and understanding both software and hardware. It covers a wide range of topics related to computer engineering.

Mathematics and Computing is a blend of math and computer knowledge. It involves not just programming and computer basics but also incorporates math-related concepts like equations, numbers, and optimizing solutions. Beyond the typical computer science subjects, it delves into areas such as financial math, applying numbers to real-world problems, and improving financial systems. Additionally, it includes theoretical computer science, mathematical modeling, simulation, and using probability and statistics to deal with chances and data. The aim is to equip students with both mathematical and computational skills to solve complex problems with the assistance of computers.


Diverse research topics and fields are available for exploration within our academic program, including Artificial Intelligence (AI), Machine Learning (ML), Big Data Analytics, Digital Image Processing, among others. This approach provides students with the opportunity to engage in their learning with enthusiasm and offers avenues for active participation and contribution to these impactful research areas.Those interested are encouraged to delve into these topics, contributing to the advancement of knowledge and innovation within the realms of AI, ML, Big Data Analytics, Digital Image Processing, and beyond.


Labs are designed such that student get to apply their theoretical concepts in practical way and wide variety of experience such as

→ Programming skill development
→Hands on experience with tools and software
→ Collaboration and teamwork
→ Prepare for industry
→ Exposure to Real-World Applications


During this 4 year of journey , some challenges which may student face which will be helpful for their betterment are

► Keeping up with technology advancement
► Work-load
► Time management
► Transition to proof based mathematics

It's about finding your way through the maze, enjoying the process, and celebrating those "aha" moments along the way.


"Every challenge is a chance to discover your strengths!"


Imagine starting your adventure in Mathematics and Computing by becoming really good at understanding how things work (that's algorithms), figuring out the language computers speak (programming languages), and solving some cool math puzzles (discrete mathematics). Now, here's the exciting part - it's not just about reading books; you actually get to do things hands-on, like making computer programs work! The skills you pick up, like using math tricks (linear algebra) and understanding chances and data (probability and statistics), help you build the foundation for making computers do amazing things, such as running programs and storing information. As you keep going, you explore some really new and exciting areas like teaching computers to learn (artificial intelligence), keeping secrets safe with codes (cryptography), and handling big sets of data (big data analytics). And it's not only about computers; you also learn how to create software, make computers easy for people to use (human-computer interaction), and use math to solve real-world problems (mathematical modeling). When you finish this journey, you're not just good at a bunch of things; you're also ready to take on challenges and grab opportunities in the always-changing world of Mathematics and Computing. It's like a journey that gets you ready for a cool job in technology!
