Overview

“Mathematics is a fundamental intellectual tool in computing, but computing is also increasingly used as a key component in mathematical problem-solving.” Mathematics and computing is an integration of mathematics with computer science. Graduates of computer science with solid mathematical foundations are currently in great demand and will remain so due to the use of computer assisted technology in our day-to-day life. The program provides comprehensive theoretical knowledge and practical training in computer science. This program will give students a formidable combination of Mathematics and Computer Science by concentrating on areas where mathematics and computing are most relevant to each other. The program has a goal to boost student's skill in math, algorithms and computation. It's all about building a robust foundation to enhance their abilities in Mathematics and Computing. With a strong grasp on math, students can tackle abstract concepts and even apply mathematical models to real-world issues. Algorithmic thinking equips them with problem-solving approaches and computational thinking helps to evaluate how efficient these solutions are. In simpler terms this course is about empowering students with the tools to understand, solve and access real-life problems through the lens of mathematics and computing. This program is designed for students aspiring to kickstart a professional journey in Mathematics and computing, right at the forefront of technology and research. By successfully completing it, student will gain crucial theoretical,technical and hands-on knowledge to take on real-world challenges. This program caters to students aspiring to build a rewarding career in Mathematics and Computing (MnC), particularly within the dynamic domains of technology, research, and development. Upon successful completion of the program, you will showcase impressive analytical, logical, and problem-solving skills.
